    Amritsar's top tourist spots

    Golden
    Temple

    This is probably the reason you are coming, but with a guide you will learn all about our beautiful temple and the Sikh religion.

    Amritsar
    Food Places

    Amritsar is world famous for its amazing food restaurants. With the Tourist Hero we will walk around the city sampling 15-18 different dishes from around 5-7 famous restaurants.

    Wagah
    Border

    Join us for the daily evening flag lowering ceremony. Both India and Pakistan soldiers meet at the border and perform the amazing ceremony.

    Jallianwala
    Bagh

    Jallianwala Bagh was the site of a tragic massacre on April 13, 1919. British troops, under General Dyer's command, opened fire on unarmed Indian protesters, killing hundreds.

    Durgiana
    Mandir

    Durgiana Mandir, is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Durga. Resembling the Golden Temple, itattracts numerous devotees for its spiritual significance and architectural beauty.

    Ram Tirath

    Ram Tirath, is an ancient pilgrimage site linked to sage Valmiki, where ita stayed and Lord Rama's sons, Lava and Kusha, were born. It holds significant religious and historical importance.

    • Experienced guide with extensive knowledge of Amritsar's places and monuments.
    • A comprehensive explanation of the history of the Golden Temple.
    • Visit the historical site of Akal Takht, also known as "The Immortal Throne."
    • Book Ceremony as the sacred scripture (SHRI GURU GRANTH SAHIB) is carried from the main shrine (Darbar) to Akal Takht.

    Witness the enchanting Golden Temple night ceremony, also known as Palki Sahib, with Tourist Hero. This sacred ritual involves the reverent transfer of the Guru Granth Sahib, the Sikh holy scripture, from the main shrine to the Akal Takht for the night. Experience the serene and spiritual atmosphere as devotees participate in kirtan (devotional singing) and the ceremonial procession. The beautifully illuminated temple and the reflective waters of the Amrit Sarovar create a mesmerizing sight, offering a unique and profound glimpse into Sikh traditions and devotion. This unforgettable experience provides a deep connection to the spiritual heart of Punjab, showcasing the Golden Temple's timeless beauty and religious significance.
